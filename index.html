DeathKematian
What happens the moment someone passes away Apa berlaku ketika seseorang meninggal dunia
All registered assets freeze instantly. Bank accounts, property, vehicles, EPF (if no nominee), investments, shares — everything under the deceased's name is locked by law. No one can sell, withdraw, or transfer until the estate is legally processed. This is harta beku.

Ownership shifts from the individual → to the estate → to be distributed among heirs (waris). Semua aset berdaftar dibekukan serta-merta. Akaun bank, hartanah, kenderaan, KWSP (jika tiada penama), pelaburan, saham — semua yang atas nama simati dikunci oleh undang-undang. Tiada sesiapa boleh jual, keluar atau pindah milik sehingga pusaka diproses secara sah. Inilah harta beku.

Pemilikan berpindah dari individu → ke pusaka → untuk diagihkan kepada waris.

Hibah
Planned lifetime transfer — exits the estate entirely before distribution Pindahan terancang semasa hidup — keluar dari pusaka sebelum agihan

Hibah is not part of the estate. If you have responsibly planned and executed a Hibah during your lifetime — through a valid akad — those assets have already been transferred to the intended recipient. They do not go through deductions, Wasiat, or Faraid. This is the essence of intentional estate planning. Hibah bukan sebahagian daripada pusaka. Jika anda telah merancang dan melaksanakan Hibah semasa hidup — melalui akad yang sah — aset tersebut sudah dipindahkan kepada penerima yang dikehendaki. Ia tidak melalui potongan, Wasiat, atau Faraid. Inilah intipati perancangan harta yang bertanggungjawab.

✓ Settled during your lifetime  ·  ✓ Goes directly to named recipient  ·  ✓ No limit on amount  ·  ✓ Can be given to anyone ✓ Diselesaikan semasa hidup  ·  ✓ Terus kepada penerima  ·  ✓ Tiada had jumlah  ·  ✓ Boleh diberi kepada sesiapa

Wasiat
Islamic will — for non-heirsWasiat 1/3 — untuk bukan waris

An Islamic will for those outside your Faraid heirs — charities, distant relatives, amal jariah. Capped at 1/3 of the remaining pusaka (after hibah assets are excluded, then after funeral costs and debts). Applies to any asset type — movable or immovable. Wasiat Islam untuk mereka di luar waris Faraid — badan amal, saudara jauh, amal jariah. Terhad 1/3 daripada baki pusaka (selepas aset hibah diasingkan, kemudian selepas kos pengebumian dan hutang). Boleh meliputi sebarang jenis aset — alih atau tidak alih.

🕌 Two Types of HibahDua Jenis Hibah

Understand the difference before choosing Fahami perbezaan sebelum memilih

✅

Hibah Mutlak

Absolute giftPemberian mutlak

The gift transfers ownership immediately upon the giver's death. Recipient becomes the legal owner with no conditions attached. Cannot be revoked after akad. Pemberian memindahkan hak milik serta-merta selepas pemberi meninggal. Penerima menjadi pemilik sah tanpa syarat. Tidak boleh ditarik balik selepas akad.

🔄

Hibah Bersyarat

Conditional giftPemberian bersyarat

The gift is conditional — for example, if the recipient dies before the giver, the asset returns to the giver. Pemberian bersyarat — contohnya, jika penerima meninggal dahulu, aset kembali kepada pemberi.
